#StayAtHomeUSC

Sharing your stories from across the USC community

The coronavirus has had an incalculable impact on our lives. From classes moving to online environments, to traveling home and leaving campus, to taking care of family, and so many other realities and responsibilites. This website provides a small outlet for sharing your stories - big and small. Our lived experiences bring us together. Let your fellow Trojans know how you are doing. Share something about how you have been impacted during the past few days and now weeks.

You can write about anything. Share a favorite place from your hometown and what it means to you during this time. Talk about your experience leaving the USC campus. Let us know how you are staying connected with friends. What are you doing to stay healthy and positive? You can share more than one story.

Each story must have a unique place so that it can be put on the map. The google form below has a place to put an address - please do NOT share your home address. Find a place in your hometown that means something to you and share that address. The location does not need to be about your story but it can be, for example a favorite hike or venue that you have spent time at.

Use this google google form to share you story. You can also use this google document template in order to more easily write your story, especially if you would like to share images.

This form can only be filled out by USC community members.
